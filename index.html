Token distribution

The more Ether everyone contributes, the more tokens you get.

The base token distribution formula is very simple: for every 1 ETH you contribute, you'll receive 100 UET2, and if you contribute at least 0.01 ETH, you'll also receive an additional 1 UET2 for every Ether that's been contributed in total. Like this:

#2 3.000 ETH 4.000 ETH 304.000 UET2
#3 0.005 ETH 4.005 ETH 0.500 UET2
#4 0.500 ETH 4.505 ETH 54.505 UET2

Bonus tokens!

Every block has a 1 in 128 chance of issuing bonus tokens.

When purchasing UET2, the smart contract will take a hash of the current block's coinbase, number, and timestamp. If the second byte of the hash is 0x00, the block becomes a bonus block. During a bonus block, the smart contract counts the number of bits set in the third byte of the hash and uses that number as a bonus multiplier.

Every UET2 purchase of at least 0.01 ETH worth during the bonus block will receive, in addition to the base distribution, an additional 100 UET2 per Ether multiplied by the bonus multiplier. That means that contributors have a chance of receiving a total of nine times the number of tokens they expected!
